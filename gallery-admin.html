Robodeck Image + GIF Gallery

Still images and every original GIF frame are converted in your browser to 64×64 RGB332. The selected GIF is downloaded completely before playback, then played using its original per-frame timing. Frames are delta-compressed when possible.

Limits: 24 gallery items and 8 MB of decoded 64×64 frame data across the gallery. GIFs are never frame-rate sampled or silently shortened; an oversized GIF is rejected instead. Use a current Chrome/Edge browser over HTTPS or localhost.

Deck controls: D-pad left/up = previous item, right/down = next item, joystick left/right = switch, joystick click = next. Hold left + right to return to the menu.
